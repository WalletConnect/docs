This page outlines the tokens and blockchains currently supported in General Availability within WalletConnect Pay. It provides a clear view of which assets can be used for:

- **Crypto settlement** — direct token transfers to merchants or customers.
- **Fiat settlement** — conversion to fiat via offramp partners before payout.

This list will be updated over time as assets move from beta into General Availability.

## Definitions

### Crypto settlement

WalletConnect Pay accepts the token from the buyer and facilitates its transfer to the merchant's or customer's wallet.

### Fiat settlement

WalletConnect Pay accepts the token from the buyer and facilitates its conversion (offramp) into fiat currency (USD, EUR) via partners, then delivers fiat to the merchant.

<Note>
Fiat settlement availability depends on offramp partner support per token and chain. Over time, fiat coverage will expand as WalletConnect Pay introduces internal swaps, allowing a broader set of tokens to be converted into supported offramp assets before payout.
</Note>
